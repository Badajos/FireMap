What happens to moose during a wildfire?

Swan Lake Fire, Alaska

On June 5th, 2019, the Swan Lake fire started in a remote region of the Kenai Peninsula, Alaska.

By late September, the fire had burned over 160,000 acres.

The Alaska Department of Fish and Game had thirteen moose tagged with GPS collars in the vicinity of the fire.
